Thoughts by Justine Pocock

about design, accessibility & life.

Passionate about making the internet better for everyone, bringing delight, happy people.

Other places on the internet to find me, Justine. Medium, Noti.st, Dribbble & Beautiful Smart Confident.

Having worked with high profile Drupal websites, such as Chatham House, University of Oxford, STEM Learning and Royal Agricultural University, I am currently leading a design team into maturity within a successful Drupal agency. This has included defining processes to ISO 9001 Quality Management System standards for many aspects of the company, including integrating design and UX into DevOps and creating a DesignOps service manual.

I am passionate about helping others excel in this industry, and have spoken at many conferences about design and mindfulness.
